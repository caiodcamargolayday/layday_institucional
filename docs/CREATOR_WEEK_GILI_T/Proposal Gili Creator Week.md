# **🌴 Proposal: Gili Creator Week – Lay Day Gili T Influencer Campaign**
## **Overview**
We propose hosting a small curated group of travel and lifestyle content creators at **Lay Day Gili T** for a 2–3 day “Creator Week” experience. (girls trip) 
The goal is to generate high-quality, reusable marketing content showcasing Lay Day Gili T and Gili Trawangan as a destination, while increasing brand awareness and driving future bookings.
## **Concept**
We will invite **5–10 selected creators** (micro to mid-tier influencers) who align with our brand aesthetic and audience.
Creators will participate in a structured content stay including:
*   Accommodation at Lay Day Gili T (shared dorms)
*   Daily experiences and group activities
*   guided content creation (photo/video)
## **Cost Structure (Controlled)**
To manage costs effectively, we propose:
### **Provided by Lay Day Gili T:**
*   Free dorm accommodation (2–3 nights)
*   1 meal per day (instead of full board)
*   2 free drinks per day 
*   Access to facilities and planned experiences ( snorkleling) 
*   hired photographer/videographer (optional depending on budget)
### **Not included:**
*   Full food coverage (only 1 meal/day provided)
*   Unlimited Alcohol or premium extras (optional upsell opportunities)
This ensures high ROI while keeping costs controlled.
## **Deliverables (Required from Creators)**
Each selected creator will agree to:
*   2- 4 short-form videos (Reels/TikTok)
*   4 – 7 Instagram Stories during stay
*   1 Instagram feed post
*   Tagging @laydaygilit on all content
*   Granting content usage rights for marketing purposes
## **Objectives / Expected Output**
This campaign is designed to generate:
*   High-quality UGC content for ads, social media, and website use
*   Organic exposure across creator audiences
*   Increased visibility of Lay Day Gili T as a social travel destination
*   Strong visual library for future marketing campaigns
## **Benefits to the Business**
*   Content created at significantly lower cost than production agencies
*   Authentic storytelling from real travelers (higher conversion than ads)
*   Multi-platform exposure (Instagram + TikTok reach)
*   Reusable content for paid advertising campaigns
*   Positioning Lay Day Gili T as a “creator-friendly destination hostel”
## **Selection Strategy**
Applicants will be screened based on:
*   Content quality (not just follower count)
*   Engagement rate
*   Audience relevance
*   Personality / group fit
This ensures brand alignment and avoids low-quality or irrelevant creators.
## **Risk Management**
*   Limited group size (5–10 people max) to maintain control
*   Pre-approved deliverables and expectations
*   Clear selection process via application form
*   No full sponsorship of extras (food/alcohol capped to reduce abuse risk)
## **Summary**
This campaign positions Lay Day Gili T as a leading creator-friendly hostel in Gili Trawangan while producing a high volume of reusable marketing content at a low production cost.
